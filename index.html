The Rainbow Serpent (Australia)

The Rainbow Serpent is an important creator figure in Aboriginal Dreamtime stories, believed to have shaped the land by forming rivers, mountains, and valleys. It is closely linked to water, life, and spiritual law.

Medusa and Athena (Greece)

A tragic story in which the Medusa is transformed by Athena into a Gorgon with snake hair and a deadly gaze, illustrating themes of punishment, power, and misunderstood strength.

Ra and the Journey Through the Underworld (Egypt)

Ra and the Journey Through the Underworld follows the sun god Ra’s nightly voyage through the Duat. Each night, he battles chaos and the serpent Apophis to ensure the sun rises again, symbolizing renewal and protection.

The Sun and the Moon (Brazil))

The Sun and the Moon is a Tupi and Guarani myth from Brazil, portraying a deep love between them. After being tragically separated, they now chase each other across the sky, explaining the cycle of day and night.

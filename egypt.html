Isis and Osiris

The story of Isis and Osiris is a tale of love and betrayal. Osiris, a wise king, is killed by his jealous brother Set. His wife, Isis, reassembles his body and revives him long enough to conceive their son Horus, starting a legacy of divine justice.

Ra and the Journey Through the Underworld

Ra and the Journey Through the Underworld follows the sun god Ra’s nightly voyage through the Duat. Each night, he battles chaos and the serpent Apophis to ensure the sun rises again, symbolizing renewal and protection.

The Weighing of the Heart

The Weighing of the Heart is an ancient Egyptian ritual where a soul's heart is weighed against Ma'at's feather. A balanced heart ensures entry into the afterlife, while a heavier heart is devoured, emphasizing moral living.

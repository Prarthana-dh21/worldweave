The Rainbow Serpent

The Rainbow Serpent is an important creator figure in Aboriginal Dreamtime stories, believed to have shaped the land by forming rivers, mountains, and valleys. It is closely linked to water, life, and spiritual law.

Tiddalik the Frog

Tiddalik the Frog, a character from Aboriginal Dreamtime stories, drank all the water in the land, causing a drought. The other animals had to come up with a clever way to make him laugh so he would release the water and restore balance to the environment.

The Emu in the Sky

The Emu in the Sky is an Aboriginal Dreamtime story depicting a dark shape in the Milky Way that looks like an emu. Formed by the dark spaces between stars, this image guides seasonal events and cultural practices for many Aboriginal groups in Australia.

The Monkey King

The Monkey King, or Sun Wukong, is a rebellious hero from "Journey to the West." Born from stone with magical powers, he causes chaos in heaven before embarking on a sacred quest, where he learns wisdom and discipline.

Chang’e – The Moon Goddess

Chang’e, the Moon Goddess from Chinese legend, drinks an elixir of immortality and floats to the moon, living in solitude and symbolizing love, loss, and the beauty of the lunar world.

Pangu and the Creation of the World

The tale of Pangu tells of a giant who emerged from a cosmic egg. He separated the sky from the earth, and after his death, his body became the mountains, rivers, and natural elements that shape our world.
